# VendiMatch: Vendor Dashboard Complete Walkthrough

Welcome to your VendiMatch Vendor Dashboard! This control center is your primary workspace for discovering new business, submitting winning proposals, managing active projects, and tracking your earnings.

Below is a detailed, section-by-section breakdown of how the Vendor Dashboard works and how you can maximize your success on the platform.

---

## 1. Dashboard (Overview)
Your main landing page provides a quick snapshot of your business health.

- **Profile Completeness**: A progress bar showing how complete your public profile is. (A 100% complete profile ranks higher in AI recommendations!)
- **Quick Metrics**: Instantly see your total **Earnings**, **Active Projects**, **Pending Proposals** waiting for buyer action, and **New Messages**.
- **Recent Activity**: A timeline feed keeping you updated on the status of your submitted quotes or incoming leads.

## 2. Service Offerings
This is your digital storefront. Buyers often search for pre-defined services rather than posting custom requirements.

- **Create Offerings**: Add clear, structured packages of the services you provide (e.g., "Full Stack Web Development", "Logo Design").
- **Pricing & Timelines**: Set fixed or hourly pricing models and estimated timelines so buyers know exactly what to expect.
- **Immediate Sync**: Any offering you publish here is immediately visible on your public vendor profile and searchable by buyers.

## 3. Leads Inbox
Your pipeline for new business opportunities. VendiMatch’s AI automatically routes relevant buyer requirements (RFPs) directly to your inbox based on your skills, category, and location.

- **Real-Time Updates**: As soon as a buyer posts a requirement matching your profile, it appears here instantly.
- **Match Scoring**: Every lead displays an **AI Match %** indicating how well your skills align with the buyer's needs.
- **Auto-Quote vs Submit Quote**: For high-match leads (>80%), you can manually craft a proposal. For lower matches, you can utilize the AI Auto-Quote feature to quickly generate a baseline proposal to save time.

## 4. Quotes
Once you submit a proposal from the Leads Inbox, it is tracked here.

- **Conversion Metrics**: Track your overall **Win Rate**, **Pending Value** (the total sum of your currently pending proposals), and your **Avg Response Time**.
- **Proposal Status**: See exactly which proposals are still **Pending** review by the buyer, which have been **Accepted** (turning into active projects), and which were **Rejected**.
- **AI Proposal Generator**: Utilize built-in AI tools to help draft compelling, professional proposals faster.

## 5. Projects
When a buyer accepts your quote, the work is managed here.

- **Escrow Security**: See the total amount the buyer has safely deposited into Escrow for your project.
- **Milestone Tracking**: View the specific deliverables and payment milestones. Once you submit the work for a milestone, the buyer will review it and release the funds.
- **Direct Chat & Disputes**: Easily jump into the project chat room with the buyer. If a disagreement occurs, you can raise a dispute to involve a VendiMatch admin.

## 6. Portfolio
A visual gallery to impress potential buyers. Upload case studies, screenshots, and PDFs of your past work to demonstrate your expertise and build trust.

## 7. Analytics
Deep dive into your profile's performance on the marketplace.
- Track how many buyers are viewing your profile over time.
- See how often you appear in search results or AI recommendations.
- Monitor your quote-to-hire conversion rates to optimize your sales strategy.

## 8. Wallet
Your financial hub. Once a buyer releases milestone payments from a project, the funds land here.
- View your available balance.
- Track upcoming payments currently held in escrow.
- Request withdrawals to your connected bank account.

---

> [!TIP]
> **Pro Tip:** Keep a close eye on the **Leads Inbox**. Being the first vendor to respond to a new requirement significantly increases your chances of winning the project. Use the AI Proposal Generator in the **Quotes** tab to respond rapidly without sacrificing quality!
