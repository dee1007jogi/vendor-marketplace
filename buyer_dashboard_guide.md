# VendiMatch: Buyer Dashboard Complete Walkthrough

Welcome to your VendiMatch Buyer Dashboard! This control center is designed to help you efficiently manage your requirements, evaluate vendor proposals, oversee active projects, and handle payments. 

Below is a detailed, section-by-section breakdown of how the Buyer Dashboard works and how you can maximize its features.

---

## 1. Dashboard (Overview)
Your main landing page provides a high-level summary of your activities on the marketplace.

- **At-a-Glance Stats**: Get instant numbers on your **Active Projects**, **Pending Quotes** requiring action, and your total **Saved Vendors**.
- **Quick Actions**: Use the prominent action buttons to quickly **Browse** the vendor marketplace or **Post a New Requirement** (RFP).
- **Recent Activity**: A feed that keeps you updated on the latest events—such as a vendor submitting a new quote or a project milestone being completed.
- **AI Recommended Vendors**: VendiMatch analyzes your past requirements and suggests highly relevant vendors that match your business needs automatically.

## 2. My Requirements
This section is your central hub for managing your Requests for Proposals (RFPs). 

- **Post New Requirement**: Create detailed briefs outlining your project needs, budget ranges, timelines, and required skills. 
- **Requirement Statuses**: Filter your RFPs by **Open** (accepting quotes), **Awarded** (vendor selected), or **Closed**.
- **Review Quotes**: For each open requirement, you can click in to see a detailed AI-powered analysis of the required skills, and navigate directly to review the incoming quotes submitted by vendors. 
- **Bulk Actions**: Select multiple requirements to **Repost** or **Archive** them simultaneously.

## 3. Shortlist
The Shortlist acts as your personal rolodex of preferred vendors. 

- **Saved Profiles**: Whenever you browse the marketplace and bookmark a vendor, they appear here. 
- **Private Notes**: Add private, internal notes to each vendor (e.g., "Great response time, keep in mind for Q3 campaign").
- **Compare Features**: Select multiple vendors from your shortlist and click **Compare Selected** to view their ratings, response times, and starting prices side-by-side to make an informed decision.
- **Direct Messaging**: Initiate a chat directly with any shortlisted vendor.

## 4. Projects & Escrow
Once you award a requirement to a vendor, it becomes an active project managed here.

- **Categorized Tabs**: Easily switch between **Active**, **Completed**, and **Disputed** projects.
- **Milestone Management**: For active projects, you can view the agreed-upon payment milestones. Once a vendor delivers the work, you can click **Approve & Pay** to securely release funds from Escrow to the vendor.
- **Delivery Files & Logs**: Access important project files (PDFs, ZIPs) uploaded by the vendor, and view a chronological log of project activities.
- **Disputes & Reviews**: If something goes wrong, you can **Raise a Dispute** which escalates the issue to VendiMatch admins. Upon successful completion, you can **Leave a Review** for the vendor.

## 5. Invoices & Payments
Your financial command center. 

- **Active Escrows**: See exactly how much money is currently held safely in VendiMatch Escrow for your active projects.
- **Transaction History**: Download PDF invoices and view a comprehensive ledger of all your past payments, deposits, and refunds.

## 6. Reviews Given
Transparency is key. This tab maintains a public record of all the feedback and star ratings you have provided to vendors upon project completion. You can review your past feedback to remember which vendors performed exceptionally well.

## 7. Disputes
A dedicated resolution center. If a project hits a roadblock and you raise a dispute in the Projects tab, it is managed here. You can track the status of open disputes, provide additional evidence to the admin team, and view the resolution history of past disputes.

## 8. Saved Searches
If you frequently hire for the same type of work (e.g., "React Developers in New York under ₹50,000"), you can save those complex filter configurations in the main marketplace. They appear here, allowing you to run the exact search again with a single click to instantly find new vendors matching those criteria.

---

> [!TIP]
> **Pro Tip:** Keep an eye on the **Pending Quotes** metric on your main dashboard. Vendors appreciate quick feedback, and maintaining a fast decision rate will make your future requirements more attractive to top-tier vendors!
